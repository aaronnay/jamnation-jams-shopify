Cheesemonger at Rainbow Grocery Cooperative

It's picnic perfection in a bite. Bright, tart-yet-sweet The Berry Last Straw strawberry jam shines next to a fluffy cloud of Sweet Alyssum, a fresh sheep's milk cheese from Garden Variety. Subtly earthy flavors in the cheese will keep you from floating away as you sit back and drink in the richness of sun-soaked summer strawberry. Bring enough to share.
